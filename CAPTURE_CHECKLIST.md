# Aradhana Catalogue — Photo Capture Checklist

Goal: every photo so consistent that one automated edit makes a perfect branded tile.
The #1 rule: **good capture = automatic clean result. Bad capture = manual fixing.**

---

## Surface & background
- [ ] Shoot on **flat matte BLACK velvet**. Gold and silver both go on black. Do **not** shoot silver on red.
- [ ] **NO wooden box, no box frame, no other items in the frame.** The wooden box edge is gold-coloured and confuses the cutout. Take the piece OUT of the box.
- [ ] Velvet clean — no lint, no big creases, no shiny glare patches.
- [ ] Avoid coloured velvet, bright trays, printed cloth, hands, boxes, and props for catalogue tiles.

## The piece
- [ ] **One item (or one matching pair) per photo.** Nothing else.
- [ ] Lay it **flat and centered**, the way it should look in the catalogue (earrings upright, ring facing up, necklace laid in a U).
- [ ] Piece fills about **60%** of the frame — leave a clear margin all around. Don't crop the edges.

## Camera / phone
- [ ] Phone on a **stand / tripod, shooting straight down** (top-down). Same height every time.
- [ ] **Lock focus & exposure** (tap and hold on the piece). Don't let it auto-change shot to shot.
- [ ] **Turn flash OFF.** Use soft, even light (lightbox or two side lamps with diffusion).
- [ ] Avoid harsh hotspots/glare on the gold — move the light, don't point it straight on.
- [ ] Wipe the lens. Hold steady / use timer so it's sharp.

## The SKU / tag (this is how the item gets named & priced)
- [ ] Shoot photos in **pairs**: first the jewellery, second its tag.
- [ ] Tag photo must be a close-up of only the tag: flat, straight, well lit, and filling the frame.
- [ ] Keep the tag out of the jewellery photo when possible. The tag photo is what names the output.
- [ ] The operator will confirm tag reads in the app whenever the tool is not fully certain.

## Consistency (do these the SAME every time)
- [ ] Same distance, same height, same lighting, same background for all 2000.
- [ ] Same orientation per category (all rings same angle, all earrings upright, etc.).

---

## Quick quality check before moving on
- [ ] Piece is sharp and fully in frame with margin.
- [ ] Background is plain black — no box, no clutter.
- [ ] No blown-out glare hiding detail.
- [ ] Next photo is the matching tag for this exact item.

If all four are ticked, the automated tool will turn it into a finished Aradhana tile with no manual work.
